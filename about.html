Hi, I'm Jeremy Yu! Welcome to my personal website. This site serves a few purposes: 1). it allows me to share my thoughts about stuff I'm interested in and 2). it is a practical personal project that I have undertaken as I continue to learn about front-end web development and dynamic web application development. So here's my website, there's more coming!

I am currently a senior at Washington University in St. Louis, majoring in computer science and sociology. I am originally from Winchester, Massachusetts, a small suburb in the Greater Boston area. In my free time, I enjoy building this website, talking about faith, talking about sports, singing, and teaching.
